Finance UI

Examples shown are external representations of submitted mockups.

Role Business Analyst, UX researcher and design
Tools Figma, Excel, Balsamiq
Date Summer 2019 - March 2021
Company Grant Thornton LLP

Research gather the present state

One of the United States' largest law enforcement agencies was still using a legacy mainframe system to manage financial transactions. Although new software had been implemented, there was a need for a central CMS that integrated everything to simplify access for the user.

At first glance of the legacy interface - it totally reminded me of the games we used to play on Macs in middle school.

my homeroom teacher was wrong. Oregon Trail totally prepared me for the real world.

thinking holistic

We mapped out the processes of all affected systems and gathered visual samples. The new user interface had to be modern but familiar to the wide range of stakeholders.

Design UI Elements

Hotlists - Priority category lists box on left hand side of screen. Circular placeholder to identify how many total items fit parameters of list.

Expand Item - Click the plus sign in the main database section to expand the item to reveal multiple entries under that voucher/deposit/case.
