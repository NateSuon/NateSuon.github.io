Support Center Redesign

During client training sessions, I noticed that many users were having issues accessing help articles or contacting us. After seeing the codebase for myself and gathering feedback from the team, I took the initiative to modernize the support center.

Role Web design and developer
Tools Bootstrap, Liquid
Date January 2018
Company Mvix Digital Signage

Mobile

One of the main requirements of this project was to allow users to access the content on a mobile device. Each page was developed to be responsive and viewable on smartphones and tablets.

    Issues with legacy site:
  • clutter - user had too many options
  • page was fixed and not fitted to display
  • items too small - users needed to zoom in to navigate page
