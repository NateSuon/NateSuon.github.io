Describe what you might do, if anything, as the product manager in the following situation: You’re working on a product team and a team member is consistently 5 minutes late to the daily stand up?

First off - does this impact the team? We can discuss with the team on changing the stand up time or find positive reinforcement to help nudge teammates in the right direction. Additionally, we could discuss with the team member privately if there are any obstacles to the stand-up time

Describe what you might do, if anything, as the product owner in the following situation: A product team has recently formed and during the first refinement meeting the team thinks that they don’t have enough ready work to begin. You dig in a little and learn that work that the team had planned to do is blocked by a 3rd party who has fallen behind schedule

Work with the 3rd party PO/SM to determine how we can work together to set more accurate sprint goals. Adjust priorities mid-sprint to swarm high-impact items
